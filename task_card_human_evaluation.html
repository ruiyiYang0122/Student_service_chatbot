01 Study Introduction

This evaluation is part of a group project conducted by Students Assistant 4 at Heriot-Watt University. The purpose is to conduct a human usability evaluation of the HWU AI Student Services Assistant.

The system is built on RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology and is designed to help HWU students find information about student services, including course registration, accommodation, scholarships, mental health support, and more.

During this evaluation, you will be asked to interact with the chatbot in a set of defined scenarios, complete a series of tasks, and record your experience. The evaluation consists of the following sections:

  • Text Interaction: Interact with the chatbot via text; record questions, responses, and ratings
  • Voice Interaction: Interact with the chatbot via voice; record questions, responses, and ratings
  • Boundary Tests: Test how the chatbot handles out-of-scope queries and privacy-sensitive topics
  • SUS Questionnaire: Complete the standardised System Usability Scale

Estimated completion time: 30 to 45 minutes.

02 Data Usage Declaration

By participating in this evaluation, you confirm that you have read and understood the following:

① All data collected during this evaluation (including your questions, responses, ratings, and feedback) will be used solely for the academic research and system improvement of this group project (Students Assistant 4). It will not be used for any commercial purpose.
② All data will be processed anonymously. Your real name will not appear in any report, presentation, or document. You will be identified only by a participant code (e.g. P01).
③ Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without giving a reason and without any negative consequences.
④ After data collection is complete, the raw data will be shared only within the group members of Students Assistant 4 and will not be disclosed to any third party.

Scenario 01 — New Student Onboarding
You are a new undergraduate student who has just received an offer from HWU and has not yet enrolled. You are completely unfamiliar with university life and do not know where to start. You need to find out what you should prepare before enrolment — such as course registration, accommodation application, and student card. Use this chatbot to find answers.
Scenario 02 — Academic Difficulties
You are a second-year postgraduate student at HWU Edinburgh Campus. You have failed a module this semester, your visa is expiring and needs renewal, and you would also like to apply for a scholarship to ease financial pressure. You have several urgent issues to resolve — use this chatbot to seek help one by one.
Scenario 03 — Stress and Confusion
You are an international student at HWU who has been going through a difficult time — your exam results were disappointing, you are having conflicts with your flatmate, and you are worried about your career prospects after graduation. You are feeling low today and want to talk to someone. You also want to know what support resources the university offers. Talk to this chatbot.
Scenario 04 — Approaching Graduation
You are a final-year HWU undergraduate student with two months until graduation. You need to confirm several graduation-related matters, including requesting an official transcript, finding out about the graduation ceremony, and setting up an alumni card. You have also heard that there is a special award for outstanding graduates, but you cannot remember the name — you want to check whether you are eligible to apply.
